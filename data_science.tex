\section{Data Science Projects}

- \cusemph{Collective and Individual Behavior in Online Communities.} Used unsupervised learning algorithm (k-means clustering) and statistical methods to discover patterns in the collective and individual behavior of innovation communities.

- \cusemph{The effectiveness of Social Sharing Practices.} Used statistical methods (multiple linear regression) to analyze the effectiveness of the ubiquitous social media sharing buttons in increasing participation in online social communities.

- \cusemph{Motivation Factors and Participation Patterns in Crowdsourcing.} Applied statistical methods to analyze motivation factors and participation patterns in crowdsourcing processes.
